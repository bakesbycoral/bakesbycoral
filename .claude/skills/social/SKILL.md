## Platform Strategy

### Instagram (@bakesbycoral) — Primary Platform

**Role:** Visual portfolio + community building + order driver
**Post frequency:** 4–5x/week (mix of feed, Stories, Reels)
**Best posting times:** Tue–Fri 11am–1pm, Sat 9–11am (Cincinnati local time)

**Content mix:**
| Type          | Frequency   | Purpose                          |
|---------------|-------------|----------------------------------|
| Feed photo    | 2–3x/week  | Portfolio, product showcase       |
| Reels         | 1–2x/week  | Process, behind-the-scenes, tips |
| Stories       | Daily       | Daily life, polls, order updates  |
| Carousel      | 1x/week     | Menu highlights, flavor guides    |

**Bio template:**
```
Gluten-Free Baking You'll Actually Crave 🍪
Custom cakes & cookies | Cincinnati, OH
100% dedicated GF kitchen | Pickup only
Order ↓
[link to /order or Linktree]
```

### TikTok (@bakes.by.coral) — Growth & Discovery

**Role:** Reach new audiences, show personality, educate about GF baking
**Post frequency:** 3–5x/week
**Best content:** Process videos, satisfying decorating, "what I baked today," myth-busting GF misconceptions

**Content ideas:**
- "Watch me decorate this cake" (satisfying process)
- "Things people say when they find out it's gluten-free"
- "A day in my home bakery"
- "How I make [flavor] cookies from scratch"
- "Wedding cake reveal"
- Trending audio + baking footage

### Facebook (Bakes by Coral) — Community & Events

**Role:** Local community, event promotion, older demographic reach
**Post frequency:** 3–4x/week
**Best content:** Order announcements, seasonal launches, customer stories, local events

### Pinterest (bakesbycoral) — SEO & Evergreen Discovery

**Role:** Long-tail search traffic, wedding/party planning inspiration
**Post frequency:** 5–10 pins/week
**Best content:** Product photos, blog posts, flavor guides, wedding cake inspiration

**Board ideas:**
- Gluten-Free Wedding Cakes
- Custom Birthday Cakes Cincinnati
- Gluten-Free Cookie Recipes & Ideas
- Celebration Cake Inspiration
- Cincinnati Local Eats

## Photo & Content Styling Tips

### Photography Guidelines

- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred — shoot near a window, morning or afternoon
- **Backgrounds:** Marble, wood, linen, or parchment — warm, neutral tones
- **Props:** Fresh flowers, scattered ingredients, branded packaging, coffee cups
- **Angles:** 45-degree for cakes, flat-lay for cookies, straight-on for tall cakes
- **Color grading:** Warm tones that match brand palette (#F7F3ED background feel)

### Video Tips (Reels / TikTok)

- Hook in first 2 seconds (show the most satisfying moment first)
- Keep it 15–30 seconds for Reels, up to 60 for TikTok
- Use trending audio when it fits naturally
- Show hands working — personal, artisan feel
- End with the finished product reveal
- Add text overlay for key info (flavor, price, "link in bio")

### Content Don'ts

- Don't use stock photos — everything should be real Bakes by Coral products
- Don't over-filter — keep it authentic and warm
- Don't post blurry or dark photos
- Don't use all caps in captions (except for one-word emphasis occasionally)
- Don't post without a caption — every image needs context

## Engagement Guidelines

- **Reply to every comment** within 24 hours — even if it's just a heart or "thank you!"
- **Reply to DMs** about orders by directing to the website: "I'd love to help! You can start your order at bakesbycoral.com/order"
- **Repost customer content** (with permission) to Stories and tag them
- **Use polls and questions** in Stories 2–3x/week to boost engagement
- **Follow and engage** with Cincinnati food accounts, local businesses, GF community accounts
